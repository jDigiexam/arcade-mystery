Game Over

At 'The Pixel Palace', the town's most popular 90s arcade, owner 'Ace' Hargrave has been found murdered in his back office.

As the detectives, you've been called to investigate. The arcade is locked down. Unravel the secrets of the staff, examine the evidence, and find the killer before they can '1-UP' you.

The Arcade Floor

You stand on the main arcade floor, the neon lights of the games casting long shadows. The suspects are waiting by the prize counter, and the crime scene is in the back office.
What's your next move?

The Suspects

  • Trisha Hargrave: The pro-gamer niece. She thinks retro games are lame and wants Ace to turn the place into a modern computer LAN hall.
  • Lacey Collins: The pushy, seductive arcade manager. She's all about getting the job done and was often seen drinking with Ace after hours.
  • 'Doc' Shaw: The wiz repair tech. He's cheap and prefers to 'Frankenstein' old machines with homemade parts rather than buy expensive new ones.
  • Stan "the man" Thompson: The rival arcade owner from downtown. His upscale building and better wages have been stealing customers and staff.
  • Liz Harding: The quiet cashier at the prize counter. There's something about her that doesn't add up...

The Crime Scene: The Back Office

The office is cluttered with spare parts and boxes of prizes. A faint smell of ozone hangs in the air. On the floor, you see a spilled bag of prize tokens and 'Ace' Hargrave's body.

Nearby on the floor lies a bloody shard of plexiglass from a broken cabinet. Next to the desk is a locked metal cash box.

A small riddle is taped to the box:

"I have a head and a tail, but no body. What am I?"

Cash Box Unlocked!

Inside the cash box, you find a printed email. It's from Hargrave to a corporate buyer, discussing the sale of the arcade. It states that his niece, Trisha, was to be given a one-time payout and completely excluded from the new company.
